Mari QM (Mariana Quinn-Makwaia) is a mixed race vocalist, composer, actor and teacher. She embraces fusion and often blends soul, jazz, rock and electronic influences with a nod to improvisation. Born and raised in the LES of NYC to an artistic family she continues to play with foundations of song, narrative and performance today. Currently, she fronts the neo-soul group 'Smoke and Sugar', joins her sister with the chamber folk group 'SYBLING', and revamps the jazz standards with 'The Lovelace Band'.
