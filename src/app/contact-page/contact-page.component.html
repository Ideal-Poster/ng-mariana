Mari QM is the solo moniker of Mariana Quinn-Makwaia, a feminist mixed race vocalist, songwriter and native New Yorker. Mariana is the singer and band leader for Smoke and Sugar, a soul/funk band that have performed all over New York City and currently have a residency at The Groove. The band self-released their debut EP 'Mindings' last year and have spent the first part of 2018 settling into a new roster of musicians while sharpening their craft. Mariana also acts as one half of the cinematic folk duo SYBLING, charmingly named after the fact that the other half of the duo is her sister. SYBLING are currently recording their first EP and are shooting a music video for their single "The Grim". As a featured vocalist, Mari QM has collaborated with artists across multiple genres ranging from electronic to jazz and beyond. Her talents and top lines shine in any musical setting she's thrown into. One of these recent projects (Vibe Mosaic) won a spot on Portland-based record label Tender Loving Empire's annual Friends and Friends of Friends Compilation. With the industry co-sign from Tender Loving Empire and a strong network of artistic collaborators in place, Mariana is set to have a break through year as Mari QM in 2019.
